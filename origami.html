ORIGAMI TUTORIAL

Origami is the art of paper folding,
which is often associated with Japanese culture.
In modern usage, the word "origami" is used as an inclusive term for all folding practices,
regardless of their culture of origin.

ORIGAMI CAMEL
There are two types of camels:
One humped or “dromedary” camels and two humped Bactrian camels.
Camels have three sets of eyelids and two rows of eyelashes
to keep sand out of their eyes.
Camels have thick lips which let them forage for thorny plants
other animals can’t eat.
